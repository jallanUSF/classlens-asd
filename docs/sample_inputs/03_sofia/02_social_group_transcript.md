        # Social Skills Group Transcript — Sofia (04/07)

        **Student:** Sofia M.  
**Date:** 2026-04-07  
**Group:** 5th grade social skills (4 students + Mr. Chen)  
**Activity:** 'What would you do?' scenario cards  
**Related IEP Goals:** G2 (perspective-taking / pragmatics)  

        ---

        **Scenario card 1:** "Your friend gets a new haircut and asks you
        what you think. You don't like it. What do you say?"

        **Mr. Chen:** Sofia, you want to start?
        **Sofia:** I would tell them the truth. If they asked, they want
        to know.
        **Mr. Chen:** Okay. What if the haircut is already done and they
        can't change it?
        **Sofia:** Then my honest answer is still honest. They should
        know for next time.
        **Peer (Marcus H.):** Dude, that's harsh.
        **Sofia:** It's not harsh. It's accurate.
        **Mr. Chen:** Sofia, how do you think your friend might feel if you
        said "I don't like it"?
        **Sofia:** [6-second pause] Probably sad. But they wouldn't be
        MORE sad than if I lied and they found out later.
        **Mr. Chen:** That's a thoughtful point. Is there a middle path?
        **Sofia:** Like what?
        **Peer (Ana):** Like, you could say "it's different from your last
        one, I bet it'll grow on me."
        **Sofia:** [long pause] That's technically not a lie. I could say
        that. [pause] But it's a little bit of a lie, because you're
        implying it might grow on you when you already know it won't.
        **Mr. Chen:** That's really sophisticated thinking, Sofia. Can we
        try practicing Ana's version?
        **Sofia:** Okay.

        **Scenario card 2:** "A classmate is crying at their desk. You
        barely know them. What do you do?"

        **Sofia:** I would leave them alone. They don't know me so it
        would be weird if I approached.
        **Mr. Chen:** What if nobody else is noticing?
        **Sofia:** Then I would tell a teacher.
        **Mr. Chen:** Good — that's a great instinct.
