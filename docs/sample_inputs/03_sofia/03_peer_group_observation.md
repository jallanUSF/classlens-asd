        # Group Work Observation — Sofia (04/09)

        **Student:** Sofia M.  
**Date:** 2026-04-09  
**Setting:** 5th grade Gen Ed, group science project (states of matter)  
**Group:** Sofia + 3 peers  
**Observer:** Ms. Patel (consulting SPED)  
**Related IEP Goals:** G3 (flexibility in group work)  

        ---

        **Task:** Groups had 30 minutes to design a poster showing states
        of matter. Each group received the same materials.

        **0:00** — Sofia immediately proposes a poster layout: "I think we
        should do a 2x2 grid — solid, liquid, gas, plasma." Peer A: "We
        only need 3 states, Mrs. K said." Sofia: "But plasma exists. We
        should include it for completeness."

        **0:04** — Peer B starts drawing a different layout (circular with
        water cycle in the middle). Sofia watches, then says "That's not
        going to work, because water cycle isn't states of matter, it's
        phase changes." **[technically correct but dismissive of peer idea]**

        **0:07** — Peer B (visibly deflated) says "Fine, you do it then."
        Sofia: [pauses] "No, I — wait. The water cycle IS phase changes,
        which IS states of matter changing. Your idea actually works. Sorry."
        **[repair — significant growth moment]**

        **0:10** — The group uses Peer B's layout with Sofia's 2x2 grid
        as a supplementary corner. Sofia volunteers to do the grid, Peer B
        does the cycle.

        **0:18** — Sofia finishes her grid. Peer A is struggling to draw
        a gas diagram. Sofia says "Want me to help? I have a way to show
        it with dots." Peer A: "Yes please."

        **0:25** — Peer C suggests they add glitter to the plasma section.
        Sofia: "Glitter isn't plasma." [2-second pause] "...but it would
        look cool. Okay." **[flexibility win]**

        **0:30** — Poster complete. Sofia presents the grid portion. Peer
        B presents the cycle. They explicitly credit each other.

        ---

        **Observer analysis:**

        - **G3 Flexibility target:** Accept at least 1 non-preferred peer
          idea per group work session.
        - **Today: 2 accepted** (water cycle layout, glitter). Both were
          preceded by initial resistance followed by self-correction or
          reconsideration.
        - **Repair moment at 0:07 is the standout.** Sofia caught her own
          dismissiveness and apologized without adult prompting. This is
          the first self-initiated social repair I've documented for her.

        **Status: Goal exceeded today.**
