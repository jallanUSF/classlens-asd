        # Independent Writing Sample — Lily (04/07)

        **Student:** Lily J.  
**Date:** 2026-04-07  
**Prompt:** Write a paragraph about an animal you find interesting  
**Setting:** Gen Ed writing block, 30 min  
**Related IEP Goals:** G1 (topic sentence + 3 supporting details)  

        ---

        **Lily's paragraph (handwritten, transcribed):**

        > Orcas are one of the most interesting animals in the ocean because
        > they are actually dolphins, not whales, and they are the top
        > predator in every ocean they live in. First, orcas live in pods
        > that are like families, and baby orcas stay with their mothers
        > for their whole lives, even when they grow up. Second, different
        > pods have different "languages" that are called dialects, and
        > scientists think these dialects are how orcas know which pod
        > they belong to. Third, orcas hunt in teams and use different
        > strategies for different prey — they make waves to knock seals
        > off ice, and they work together to herd fish into tight balls.
        > These three things show that orcas are extremely smart and social,
        > almost like the ocean version of wolves. In Minecraft I built
        > an aquarium with orcas in it, and even though Minecraft orcas
        > don't do any of this, it's still my favorite mob.

        **Structure analysis:**
        - Topic sentence: ✅ "Orcas are one of the most interesting animals
          in the ocean because they are actually dolphins..."
        - Detail 1: ✅ "live in pods that are like families" (with elaboration)
        - Detail 2: ✅ "different pods have different languages/dialects"
        - Detail 3: ✅ "hunt in teams and use different strategies" (with
          2 specific examples)
        - Concluding sentence: ✅ "These three things show that..."
        - Tangent at the end: the Minecraft reference. Ms. Delgado left
          it because it demonstrates personal connection, but it breaks
          the structural pattern.

        **Rubric (G1 target):**
        - Topic sentence: 4/4
        - 3+ supporting details: 4/4
        - On-topic: 3/4 (Minecraft tangent)
        - Organization: 4/4

        **Goal status: MET for the week.** This is Lily's third consecutive
