## 📝 Implementation Notes for Educators

### IEP Alignment
The tasks within the "First" section are specifically designed to target social initiation beyond basic fact-sharing. By requiring a peer-focused question and a reciprocal follow-up ("Why/How"), Sofia is practicing genuine social reciprocity and active listening rather than reciting data.

### Interest Integration
The "Global Explorer" theme and the geography puzzle app reinforcer leverage Sofia's high motivation for maps and categorization, increasing the likelihood of task completion and engagement.

### Sensory Integration
The inclusion of "rhythmic tapping" serves as a transitional sensory break. This allows Sofia to meet her need for rhythmic input following the cognitive and emotional effort of social interaction, aiding in self-regulation before transitioning to the reward.

### Communication Support
To reduce the anxiety associated with social initiation, the board provides a concrete sentence starter (*"What did you do this weekend?"*). This scaffolding supports Sofia in bridging the gap between the desire to interact and the execution of the social skill.
