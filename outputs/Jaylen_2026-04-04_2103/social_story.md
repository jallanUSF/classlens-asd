# Social Story: Saying Hello to My Friends

**Student:** Jaylen  
**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Grade:** 1  

---

## My Morning Story

Every morning, Jaylen goes to school. 

The school bell rings to start the day. 

Jaylen sees his friends in the classroom. The classroom is a place where friends play. 

Jaylen likes to think about Gordon and Percy. His friends are like Percy—friendly and kind. 

When friends see Jaylen, they feel happy. Friends might say, "Hi Jaylen!"

**How I Can Say Hello:**
* I can wave my 5 fingers to say hello.
* I can use my AAC device to say "Hi" back.
* I can press the "Hi" button on my device.
* I can use PECS to say hello.

Greeting friends is like a green light to start the day. 

Friends feel glad when Jaylen greets them. They like to see Jaylen's smile.

Teachers like it when friends are kind to each other. 

If I feel nervous, it is okay to take a deep breath before greeting a friend.

**Jaylen is a great friend!**
