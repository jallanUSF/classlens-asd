## 🛠 Implementation Notes for Staff

### AAC Support & Prompting
* **Proximity:** Ensure the AAC device is within arm's reach before presenting the board to the student.
* **Prompt Hierarchy:** If Jaylen struggles with a request, provide a partial prompt (e.g., gesturing toward the "Request" folder) to support the 80% accuracy target while encouraging independence.

### Sensory Integration
* **Regulated Transitions:** The "5 deep pressure squeezes" are strategically placed between the two AAC tasks. This provides the necessary vestibular and proprioceptive input Jaylen requires to regulate his system and refocus for the final request.

### Reinforcer Delivery
* **Contingency:** Provide the Thomas trains and spinning top immediately upon the completion of the final AAC request.
* **Duration:** Provide the reward for exactly 2 minutes. It is recommended to use a visual timer to provide a clear beginning and end to the reinforcement period.
