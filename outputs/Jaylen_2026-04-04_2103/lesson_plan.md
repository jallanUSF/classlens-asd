# Lesson Plan: The Railway Request Adventure

**Student:** Jaylen  
**Grade:** 1 (ASD Level 3)  
**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Estimated Duration:** 30 Minutes  

---

## 🎯 Instructional Overview
**Goal ID:** `communication_requesting_AAC_01`  
**Objective:** Jaylen will request wants and needs (specifically trains, quantities, and sensory input) using his AAC device to align with his IEP goal of 80% accuracy across daily opportunities.  
**Interest Integration:** This lesson leverages Jaylen's high interest in "The Railway Station," specifically Gordon and Percy trains, and incorporates the number 5 for quantity requesting.

---

## 🛠 Materials & Resources
*   **AAC Device:** Personalized device with "Request" and "Sensory" folders
*   **Visuals:** Laminated (non-paper texture) visual choice board with icons for "Gordon," "Percy," "Spin," and "Squeeze"
*   **Manipulatives:** Gordon and Percy train figures, wooden train track setup, and a spinning top
*   **Tools:** Visual/Digital timer

---

## 👣 Instructional Sequence

### 1. Warm-Up: Sensory Primer
**Duration:** 2 Minutes  
To regulate Jaylen's system and prepare him for focused engagement, begin with a sensory primer consisting of deep pressure squeezes or a body sock session.

### 2. Main Activity: "The Station Master"
In this structured game, Jaylen takes on the role of the Station Master. To move the trains along the track, Jaylen must use his AAC device to request:
*   **Specific Characters:** Gordon or Percy.
*   **Quantities:** Requesting a specific number of items (e.g., "5 trains").
*   **Sensory Input:** Requesting deep pressure breaks.

### 3. Guided Practice
*   **Modeling:** The adult will first model the request for Gordon the Tank Engine using the AAC device.
*   **Prompting:** The adult will then prompt Jaylen to request Percy using a gestural or hand-over-hand prompt.
*   **Fading:** Support will be systematically faded as Jaylen demonstrates success.

### 4. Independent Practice
Jaylen will be presented with a choice of three highly preferred items:
1.  Spinning top
2.  Gordon train
3.  Body sock  
**Requirement:** Jaylen must use his AAC device to request one of these items without any adult prompting.

---

## 🪜 Scaffolding & Support

| Support Category | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Prompt Hierarchy** | Gestural (pointing to device) $\rightarrow$ Partial Physical (touching elbow) $\rightarrow$ Full Physical (hand-over-hand) |
| **Environmental** | Dim overhead lights to prevent sensory overload |
| **Visuals** | Utilize high-contrast icons on the AAC device |

---

## 📊 Assessment & Data Collection
**Data Tracking:** The teacher will record the number of independent requests versus prompted requests using the AAC device.

**Success Criteria:** 
*   **Accuracy:** Selection of the correct icon to express a want/need.
*   **Latency:** Selection must occur within 10 seconds of the prompt.
