## 💡 Implementation Notes

### Sensory Integration
The **"Raptor Presses"** are strategically placed to provide necessary proprioceptive input and deep pressure. This assists Maya in regulating her sensory system and increasing her readiness for the rewarding "Then" activity.

### Goal Scaffolding
To support Maya's communication goals while respecting her current comfort level, the greeting task is phrased as **"Wave or Say."** This allows her to successfully engage in a peer greeting via gesture if verbal communication is challenging at that moment.

### Engagement Tip
To foster autonomy and a sense of accomplishment, allow Maya to use a **purple marker** to check off each completed box.
