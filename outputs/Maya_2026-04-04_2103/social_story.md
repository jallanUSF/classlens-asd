# Social Story: Saying Hello to My Friends

**Student:** Maya  
**Grade:** 3  
**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Goal:** Greeting peers upon school arrival

***

Every morning, I go to school. I love the color purple.

When I arrive, I see my friends in the hallway. My friends are happy to see me!

My friends might say, **"Hi Maya!"** They might wave their hand at me. When friends say hi, they are being kind. They want to be my friend.

I can smile at my friends. I can say **"Hi"** back to them. Some friends use short words to say hello, and a wave is also a friendly way to say hi. I can use my words or I can use a wave.

It is okay to take a moment to think. 

Sometimes it feels very busy in the hall. When it feels busy, I can count my friends just like I count my raptors. 

**One... two... three friends!**

Counting helps me feel calm and ready. 

Blue the Raptor is a brave raptor. I can be brave just like Blue and say hello. 

My friends feel glad when I respond to them. Greeting my friends makes them smile. 

Now, I can walk to class with my friends. It is a great way to start my day!
