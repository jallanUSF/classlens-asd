## 🛠️ Implementation & Teacher Notes

### Communication Support
Maya currently utilizes 3-4 word phrases. To support the **"Say hello!"** step, provide a sentence starter card if she experiences hesitation:
*   *"Hi [Name], how are you?"*

### Sensory Considerations
To ensure the effectiveness of the **"Quiet calm time,"** please verify the following:
*   The designated area is located away from primary fluorescent lighting.
*   Noise-canceling headphones are readily available if the morning classroom volume is elevated.

### Goal Tracking & Reinforcement
*   **Immediate Feedback:** When Maya successfully completes the "Say hello!" step, mark the tally on her Tracking Sheet immediately to reinforce the positive social behavior.
*   **Transition Strategy:** The "Count my things" step is intentionally placed to utilize Maya's interest in counting and raptors, serving as a predictable bridge between the social demand of greeting and the academic demand of desk work.
